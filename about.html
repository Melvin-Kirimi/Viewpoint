HISTORY:

 Founded in 1914, Viewpoint University emerged from a vision to cultivate intellectual curiosity and societal advancement in Viewpoint after a major setback due to the effects of the first world war. Initially a modest institution focused on integrity and discipline, its early years were marked by a dedicated faculty and a pioneering spirit. By 1960, a pivotal expansion introduced a vast of faculties like Computing, Engineering and Business and Economics establishing the university's commitment to diverse academic pursuits.

The mid-20th century witnessed Viewpoint's transformation into a research-intensive hub, fostering groundbreaking discoveries in Technology advancements that pioneered the revolution in technology of Viewpoint. The university's engagement with the community deepened through community service programs, job creation, mass education on self empowerment and offering education on vocational skills. A surge in student diversity during 2000 enriched the campus culture, reflecting the evolving global landscape.

Today, Viewpoint University stands as a leading institution, renowned for its academic excellence, innovative research, and commitment to social responsibility. Its graduates contribute significantly to the business world and transformation of technology in Viewpoint.
